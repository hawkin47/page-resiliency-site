Who We Are

Founded by Sarah Page, Page Resiliency Solutions is a boutique consulting firm with a mission to transform how organizations anticipate, prepare for, and respond to disruptive events. With more than 13 years of specialized experience in disaster recovery, Sarah has delivered strategic guidance and operational execution for some of the world’s most recognized enterprises and public institutions.

Sarah Page is a Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity expert known for her strategic insight and calm leadership under pressure. Her background includes leading enterprise-wide resiliency initiatives at Stanley Black & Decker, Willis Towers Watson, and the City of Seattle.
